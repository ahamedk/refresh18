About the event

Freshworks brings to you - Refresh 18! Gear up for 2 days of unlimited learning, inspiring solutions, networking and fun.

Packed with over 45 knowledge-sharing sessions, Freshworks’ first user conference is here to help you succeed in your role, get hands-on with the product, connect with inspiring minds and refresh yourself!

Engage in sessions about the latest trends and developments in helpdesk/customer support, customer experience management, IT service desk, collaboration and communication. Redefine your career with our learning sessions, certification opportunities, hands-on demos and other solutions to help accelerate your growth.

Why Attend

Get the exclusive scoop first

Get exclusive scoop on our latest product features, updates, and new products ahead of time. Learn about what we have in store for you, with our product roadmap sessions. Gather the best practices and success stories that can transform your business

Indulge in godspeed networking

Refresh 18 is the perfect opportunity to connect with inspiring peers and industry thought leaders. It’s an ideal platform to discover imperative techniques and ideas, from those who have been there and done that. Indulge in curating ideas that will take your business to the next level.

Get certified & climb your way up

Become a Freshworks Certified Professional through our product learning sessions, practical how-to’s and real-world solutions. Hone your skills, elevate your career and climb your way up the ladder. Meet with our product managers and experts, for one-on-one deep learning sessions.

Limitless solutions

Meet with Freshworks’ Marketplace and Partners, and test drive the solutions and products offered by them. Learn how to extend the capabilities of our suite of products with powerful integrations and apps that help you get the most value from them.

Have fun
